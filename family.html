The Akol Family Tree

Three generations of the Akol family — rooted in Nyero Subcounty, Kumi District, Eastern Uganda

Papa Kelment Akol

FAMILY PATRIARCH

The foundation of the Akol family. A respected elder who has guided the family through generations with wisdom, faith, and strong Iteso values.

Tata Akiteng Phoebe

FAMILY MATRIARCH

The heart of the Akol family. Her love, prayers, and guidance have nurtured the family through joys and challenges across generations.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Children of Papa Kelment & Tata Phoebe

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

1. Papa Akol Stephen

👶 14 Children
📋 About this branch: Papa Akol Stephen is the eldest child of Papa Kelment Akol and Tata Akiteng Phoebe. He has been blessed with 14 children who are spread across Uganda. His family is known for their strong work ethic, dedication to education, and active participation in community development in Kumi District.
👩‍👧‍👦

2. Ija Acham Anna Phoebe

👶 6 Children
📋 About this branch: Ija Acham Anna Phoebe is the second child of Papa Kelment Akol and Tata Akiteng Phoebe. She has been blessed with 6 children. Her family is known for their strong faith, hospitality, and dedication to serving the community through church and social activities.
👩‍👧‍👦

3. Ija Alupo Norah

👶 9 Children
📋 About this branch: Ija Alupo Norah is the third child of Papa Kelment Akol and Tata Akiteng Phoebe. She has been blessed with 9 children. Her family is recognized for their entrepreneurial spirit, with many members engaged in trade and business across Eastern Uganda.
🕊️

4. Papa Omoding Joseph Benjamin 🕊️ Rest in Peace

👶 5 Children
📋 About this branch: Papa Omoding Joseph Benjamin was the fourth child of Papa Kelment Akol and Tata Akiteng Phoebe. Though he is deceased, his legacy lives on through his 5 children. His family continues to honor his memory through their commitment to education and community service.
👩‍👧‍👦

5. Ija Amuge Dinah

👶 8 Children
📋 About this branch: Ija Amuge Dinah is the youngest child (5th child) of Papa Kelment Akol and Tata Akiteng Phoebe. She has been blessed with 8 children. Her family is known for their warmth, unity, and dedication to preserving Iteso cultural traditions.
